üìä Game Mechanics

Movement System

  • All blocks move together in the direction you choose
  • Blocks slide until they hit a wall or another block
  • Plan your moves carefully - you have limited moves per level!

Scoring System

  • Basic Score: 10 points per block removed
  • Chain Multiplier: 2+ chains multiply your score (2-chain = 2x, 3-chain = 3x, etc.)
  • Combo Bonuses: Large matches give special combo messages
  • Move Bonus: Unused moves contribute to your final score

Special Features

  • Reset Position: Costs moves but shuffles all blocks to new positions
  • Auto-Generation: Lonely single blocks automatically generate pairs (penalty applies)
  • Chain Reactions: Clearing blocks can cause cascading matches
